# GSRS Visual Identity Standard

STATUS: ACTIVE CANDIDATE
VERSION: 2026-06-25

## Governing Principle

GSRS visual identity is a controlled system. Logo, typography, color, layout, spacing, asset presentation, and atmosphere must remain consistent across website, print, packaging, marketing, ambassador materials, email, and future applications.

## Brand Atmosphere

Premium. Earthy. Quiet. Curated. Private-gallery. Relationship-centered. Trustworthy. Story-rich. Handcrafted. Not mass retail.
